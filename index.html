FAQ

frequently asked questions

What's your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 300–500 pcs depending on the product complexity. For custom molds, MOQ starts at 1,000 pcs. We're happy to accommodate trial orders for new clients — just ask!

How long does custom mold making take?

Simple molds: 3–5 working days. Complex multi-cavity molds: 7–10 working days. Rush service available for urgent projects — contact us for availability.

What materials do you work with?

PVC (clear, colored, anti-static), PET (food-grade, recyclable), PP, HIPS, and more. Thickness range: 0.2mm to 10mm. Tell us your application and we'll recommend the best material.

Do you provide free samples?

Yes! We provide free existing samples. Custom mold samples require a small mold fee (refunded on first bulk order). Customers cover shipping cost.

What shipping methods do you offer?

Sea freight (most economical for bulk), air freight (7–10 days), and express courier DHL/FedEx/UPS (3–5 days). We can ship to your freight forwarder or arrange door-to-door delivery.

What payment terms do you accept?

T/T (bank transfer), L/C at sight, Western Union, and Alibaba Trade Assurance for first orders. Standard terms: 30% deposit, 70% balance before shipment or against B/L copy.
